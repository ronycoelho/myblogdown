About

I am a Brazilian Political Scientist working mainly with political participation of civil society in public policies. I have worked as a Visiting Researcher at the Institute of Development Studies (IDS, from Nov 2018 to Nov 2019), University of Sussex, and have done a pos-doc in the Project “Participação Política – Procad (Unesp/UEM/UFSC)”. I have experience working with quantitative data, using R Programming language, and qualitative data, using Nvivo.

If you would like to know more about my work, please, visit my academic Curriculum Vitae (CV) and my Researchs on this site (Research). If you would like to contact me, please, just send me an email: coelhorgs@yahoo.com.br
